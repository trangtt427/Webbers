Acuity x Airbnb

I led the design of an integration that allows Acuity customers to make their services bookable on Airbnb.

Background

Acuity entered a partnership with Airbnb that would allow travellers to book Squarespace services on Airbnb.

In what was described as their biggest release of the summer, the revamped Experiences and Services, Airbnb would integrate with Acuity Scheduling so that Squarespace users can sync up their services and list them in Airbnb's vetted marketplace geared towards travelers. Acuity would get a cut of the revenue of booked Airbnb services as a result.

Solutions

I designed the entire user journey of this integration, starting from eligible users discovering this feature to syncing their appointment types over to Airbnb.
