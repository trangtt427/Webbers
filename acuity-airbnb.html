Squarespace

Acuity x Airbnb Integration

Led the design of a first-of-its-kind integrated product experience that allows Squarespace sellers to list their services on Airbnb.

Background

Acuity entered a partnership with Airbnb that would allow Squarespace sellers to make their services available on Airbnb.

In what was described as their biggest release of the summer, the revamped Experiences and Services, Airbnb would integrate with Acuity Scheduling so that Squarespace users can sync up their services and list them in Airbnb's vetted marketplace geared towards travelers. Acuity would get a cut of the revenue of booked Airbnb services as a result.

Solutions

I designed for the entire user journey of this integration, starting from eligible users discovering this feature to syncing their appointment types over to Airbnb.

A self-serve user invite and management workflow

Several new features to support multi-location scheduling and organization-level management

The Enterprise Dashboard, a product exclusive to Org Managers to oversee their scheduling operations

Single Sign-on flows for 3 major providers
