About Me

A designer with 3+ years of visual, UX, and product design. Currently, I'm working at Incorta where I am designing a no-ETL platform that helps users make use of their data fast and effortlessly. Prior to this, I designed SaaS products for an IoT buildings automation company called Controlco, created Bio-Rad's first fully responsive library and web/mobile experience, and led the UX and visual design efforts for Engagio's marketing campaigns.

As an artist and product designer, I love taking a creative approach to problems. I can easily immerse myself in exploration to come up with innovative ideas, but I also allow my designs to be data-driven. When I'm not working, I'm usually traveling or planning for my travels, trying out new dishes or cooking them, drawing, or playing with my Nintendo Switch.

Side note: I built this whole website from scratch. I do some front-end development here and there, although I'd like to spare your eyes from my code monstrosities.
