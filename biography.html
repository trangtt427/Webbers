Accessibility and inclusion by way of design.

I believe that no matter the industry or audience, people should feel at ease when confronted with technology. My resolve is to create useful and beautiful interfaces that lead to positive experiences for users. I'm here to help remove the intimidation of having to learn something new on our devices.

My passions include making art, traveling, and learning. You can find me working on one of my digital paintings while listening to lo-fi chill beats, or planning for my next travel. Nothing inspires me more than seeking out new vibes, experiences, and knowledge.

Thanks for dropping in and feel free to hit me up if you want to chat.
