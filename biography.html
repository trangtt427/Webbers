About

Hi, I'm Trang.

I’m a product designer on a mission to help people overcome technology through design solutions backed up by empathy, creativity, and data.

What got me into design? Playing the Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past for the first time back in the 90s. The 8-bit music, the graphics, the storytelling — everything was strung together beautifully to deliver a captivating experience that influenced my take on art, design, and technology. Over two decades later, I'm now a designer working to tell a different story, one that still draws upon the knowledge bred from that experience, to let people know that technology can be delightful as they are useful.

My designs have had an impact in enterprise, startups, biotech, fintech, data analytics, and IoT.

I'm comfortable shepherding features through the entire design process, from defining/discovering user needs to crafting pixel-perfect visuals. I am a huge advocate of users, collaboration, and creative exploration, and you'll notice this as I ask questions to learn more about the problem space and push for solutions that put users at the helm. In the end, the design is only impactful if it helps users get their jobs done. Otherwise, it’s just expensive pixels and code.

Whether it's hiking a nearby trail or traveling to a foreign country, I seek out good vibes so I can stay inspired and connected to the world around me. My wanderlust is partially driven by my adventurous palate. As cheesy as it may sound (I like puns), food plays a huge part in my identity and provides a unique way to bond with others. I also enjoy indie music, art, illustrating on my tablet, playing my Nintendo Switch, and I like befriending every stray cat in my neighborhood.

Thanks for dropping by. Feel free to send a message if you're curious about me.
