About Me

As someone who has worked with patients and healthcare in a past life, it's fulfilling to be able to work as a product designer today, where I get to empathize and make a direct impact on people's lives via design. I have 3+ years of industry experience in both start-up and large companies. Some of my biggest achievements include having built a SaaS software for IoT automation, leading design efforts for marketing, creating and maintaining design systems, and shipping features for an innovative data analytics platform.

When I'm not designing, you can find me drawing, trying out new restaurants, learning how to cook new dishes, playing my Nintendo Switch, hiking, and/or planning for my next big adventure!
