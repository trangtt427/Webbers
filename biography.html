About Me

If you were able to get to this page, it's safe to assume that at some point in your life, you've experienced joy or frustration using a digital product. I know exactly how it feels. I enjoy immersing myself in design to help facilitate these experiences so that people like you and I can get things done effortlessly and with peace of mind.

I am inspired by many things: art, movies, games, traveling, interactions with different types of folks, and most importantly, my curiosity. Being inspired and curious truly helps me thrive as a designer.

And yes, I built this whole website from scratch. I do some front-end development here and there, although I'd like to spare your eyes from my code monstrocities.
