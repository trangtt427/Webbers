Kawaii Food Icons
Visual Design | Illustration

Project Overview

To keep my illustration skills fresh, I'm currently making a sticker set of my favorite foods— with cute faces. These faces are supposed to be reminiscent of the Japanese art style you'd see at the Daiso stores. I'm planning to make them into digital stickers for messaging, as well as physical decals. They're created using Adobe Illustrator. Check back in a bit to see what the printed stickers look like.

Top left to right: Cookie, Taco, Ramen, and Bao. Bottom left to right: Ice Cream, Hambugga, Nigiri.
