About My Art

I enjoy drawing and painting digitally mainly because I don't have to deal with buying the actual art supplies anymore. I do carry a notebook with me wherever I go to doodle though. I always challenge myself by drawing in different styles and themes. My inspiration for my artwork comes from almost anything and everything, whether it's things I see during my travels, pop culture, movies, or games.
