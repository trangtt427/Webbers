I've designed through 10 years of paradigm shifts, adapting to changes in technology and human needs. I'm an analytical designer with lots of range. I enjoy running point on research, digging into complex domains, thinking at a systems level, and translating behind-the-scenes complexity into elegant solutions that make a difference in people's lives. My approach to delivering product excellence is to fixate on details, clarity, and purpose, and keep designs rooted in simplicity and intention.

Currently, I'm designing at Squarespace to help small business owners design how they show up in the world, while accelerating a new way of working in an AI-forward product development process. Outside of work, I'm a wannabe chef and sometimes cat photographer for an adoption non-profit.
