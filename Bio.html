The Design Journey

During my early childhood, I was often seen with scissors cutting up paper and drawing on my walls with crayons. I found myself at a fork as I got older, wanting to work in the field of medicine to help others yet wanting to keep alive my creative aspirations. Despite going hard in the paint towards becoming a healthcare professional, my passion for art came full circle.

Artistic Beginnings

As a child, I was drawing, painting, building, and doing things that generated a lot of creativity. I was even featured on TV for winning a county-wide art contest when I was 11 years old.

Undergrad Biology Years

In school, I became interested in genetics, medicine, human disease and psychology. I enjoyed learning about what makes up our bodies chemically, what makes us behave the way we do, and how we interact with others. I graduated with a B.S. in Biology from UCSB.

Visual Design

Even while pursuing Biology, I kept my creative chops fresh with art courses and designed digital collateral whenever I got the chance. Throughout college and after, I took on various freelance projects. It was clear that my strength and passion lie in visual design, so I made the shift.

Front End Talk

I got into designing for web technologies and learned more about the world of development. During this time, I taught myself how to write HTML/CSS and use industry standard design tools.

UX/UI Design

I began taking a holistic approach to design, focusing on every step of the process. I kept users at the forefront of my brain and made sure that my design was empathetic to their needs. To fortify my foundation, I enrolled in a one year program at UCSD to receive my User Experience Design Certificate.

UX design is the culmination of my lifelong pursuit of science and creativity. When people think about design, the first thing that comes to mind are the pretty visuals. UX design is more of a science; it's built on research, data, and testing, which I've become familiar with throughout studying Biology. It's exciting to be able to pick apart problems and provide creative solutions to help people in the digital space.
