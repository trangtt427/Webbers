Making 401(k)s Accessible for People in All Lines of Work

Human Interest is a modern, full-service 401(k) provider for small and mid-sized businesses. On a mission to close the retirement gap in America, Human Interest has taken strides to lower the barrier of entry for people to invest in their future. The company has done this through:

  • Removing one-time transaction fees for plans and
  • Building in-house recordkeeping technology to provide better end-to-end experiences for their customers (most 401(k) providers pay a third-party for this type of service)

As a senior product designer, I'm embedded in a lot of mission-critical projects, contributing to research, planning and design execution. I team up with different parts of the org to understand the complex innerworkings of the whole system.

Internal Tools

My very first projects at the company were complex internal tools that involve money movement. I supported internal teams (Operations, Recordkeeping, Implementation, etc.) and probed into areas of inefficiencies. The theme during this phase was automation and the goal was to bring that into our solutions and save our teams from redundant, manual work. One notable product I delivered was a tool to deconvert plans efficiently, a critical business operation we've never been able to do before.

Admin and Participant Experience

Most of my design efforts are spread across the Admin and Participant Experiences. I lead design on the Admin Experience, currently focusing on improving their onboarding experience and guiding them through high-stakes decision-making flows. I also work on post-onboarding initiatives such as the Admin Dashboard to help admins manage their company's 401(k). For the Participant Experience, I design around business goals like increasing participation rates and reducing cost-to-serve, while bringing value to participants through improved quality of life features in their app.

Evolving Design at Human Interest

Outside of business related objectives, I work to fortify Design's presence at the company. I've worked alongside Leadership to develop core Design competencies to execute at the highest level of craft and to align on how we hire designers. As a lover of efficiency, I always try to find ways to improve design operations and relationships in other areas of the business.

Due to privacy reasons, I can't show much of the work I've done. If you'd like to hear more about what I've been up to at Human Interest, feel free to reach out.
