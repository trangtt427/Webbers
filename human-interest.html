Making 401(k)s Accessible for People in All Lines of Work

Human Interest is a modern, full-service 401(k) provider for small and mid-sized businesses. On a mission to close the retirement gap in America, Human Interest has taken strides to ensure a low barrier of entry for people who want to invest in their future. The company has done this through:

  • Removing one-time transaction fees for customers and
  • Building in-house recordkeeping technology to provide better end-to-end experiences for their customers (most 401(k) providers pay a third-party for this type of service)

As a senior product designer, I'm embedded in a lot of mission-critical projects, offering contributions to research, planning and design execution. I partner closely with a ton of people across different parts of the business to understand the complex innerworkings of the whole system.

Internal Tools

My very first projects at the company were complex internal tools that involve money movement. I worked hand in hand with various internal teams (Operations, Recordkeeping, Implementation, etc.) to understand where we they were struggling. The theme during this phase was automation and the goal was to bring that into our solutions so that teams weren't under water with redundant tasks. One notable product I delivered was a tool to deconvert plans efficiently, a critical business operation we've never been able to do before.

Admin and Participant Experience

Most of my design efforts are spread across the Admin and Participant Experiences. I lead design on the Admin Experience, currently focusing on improving their onboarding experience and guiding them through high-stakes decision-making flows. I also work on post-onboarding initiatives such as the Admin Dashboard to help admins get what they need to manage their company's 401(k). For the Participant Experience, I design around business goals like increasing participation rates and reducing cost-to-serve, while bringing value to our participants through quality-of-life features in their app.

Evolving Design at Human Interest

Outside of business related objectives, I also work to fortify Design's presence at the company. I've worked with Leadership to develop core Design competencies to execute at the highest level of craft and to align on how we hire designers. As a lover of efficiency, I always try to find ways to improve the way design collaborates with other parts of the org.
