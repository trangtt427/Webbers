Human Interest

401(k)s for Small and Mid-sized Businesses

Helping close America's retirement gap by empowering people with 401(k)s that are simple, delightful, and affordable.

Summary

Designed a unified tooling hub that simplified internal operational workflows and serves as the source of truth for customer data

Shipped a feature for the internal recordkeeping team to liquidate plan assets in bulk

Evolved the company Design competencies and established standards around hiring

role

Senior Product Designer

timeframe

April 2020 - Present

application

Web + Mobile Web

opportunity

Making 401(k)s Accessible for People in All Lines of Work

Human Interest is a modern, full-service 401(k) provider for small and mid-sized businesses. On a mission to close the retirement gap in America, Human Interest has taken strides to lower the barrier of entry for people to invest in their future. The company has done this through:

  • Removing one-time transaction fees for plans and
  • Building in-house recordkeeping technology to provide better end-to-end experiences for their customers (most 401(k) providers pay a third-party for this type of service)

As a senior product designer, I'm embedded in a few top company initiatives, contributing to research, planning and design execution. I often lend my efforts to the research and knowledge-building of complex business areas and paint the bigger picture to bring teams clarity around users and the business.

Internal Tools

My very first projects at Human Interest involved building proprietary tools to streamline money movement. I supported internal teams (Operations, Recordkeeping, Implementation, etc.) and probed into the current inefficiencies. The theme for this area was automation, and the goal was to bring that into our solutions and save our teams from redundant, manual work. One of my fondest memories at the company was when I collaborated with the Recordkeeping team to create a tool for deconverting plans, a critical business operation we've never been able to do before.

Admin and Participant Experience

Most of my design efforts are spread across the Admin and Participant Experiences. I lead design on admin onboarding to guide users through high stakes decision-making with a pleasant, intuitive experience. For the Participant Experience, I design around business goals like increasing participation rates and reducing cost-to-serve, while bringing value to participants through improved quality of life features in their app.

Evolving Design at Human Interest

Outside of business initiatives, my resolve is to fortify Design's presence at the company. I've worked alongside Leadership to develop core Design competencies to execute at the highest level of craft and to align on how we hire designers. As a lover of efficiency, I always try to find ways to improve design operations and relationships in other areas of the business.

Due to privacy reasons, I can't show much of the work I've done. If you'd like to hear more about what I've been up to at Human Interest, feel free to reach out.
