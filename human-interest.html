overview

Good-bye Third Party, hello in-house Recordkeeper.

In 2015, Human Interest started building their own 401(k) recordkeeping technology to manage all plans and transactions in-house. Today, Human Interest is tackling a company-wide initiative of migrating all of their plans from their third-party custodian over to their in-house recordkeeping platform. This initiative would improve customer experience, provide automated solutions to longwinded manual processes, and most importantly, increase blended gross margins to make Human Interest a self-sustaining software company. Some of the things I work on:

  • Scalable solutions to semi-automate the process of trading
  • Loan management platform
  • Tooling to allocate funds to participants
  • Apply a design approach to rethink outdated and manual retirement processes
  • Facilitating design workshops to uncover user challenges

As the Design Lead for the Platform side of the product, I partner very closely with Product to identify/research our gaps and opportunities. We start by gathering the data to support these opportunities, through the help of the Analytics team and Ops teams. I'm responsible for stringing across the different functions of our company to think of holistic cost-efficient solutions end-to-end.

This is only the tip of the iceberg. If you want to hear the juicy details about my role at Human Interest, feel free to reach out! We can work out something that is NDA-compliant.
