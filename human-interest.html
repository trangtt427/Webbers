overview

Good bye third party company, hello in-house recordkeeper.

In 2015, Human Interest started building their own in-house technology to manage all 401(k) plans and transactions. This company-wide initiative would improve customer experience, provide automated solutions to longwinded manual processes, increase gross margins, and set Human Interest apart from other 401(k) providers.

Here are some of the things I've done and will continue to work on:

  • Tooling that can execute trades and disburse participants for distributions
  • Scalable solutions to semi-automate the processing of deposits
  • Designed an application that allows the creation of loan parameters and tracking
  • Came up with an interface for allocation of dividends and funds

Design partners with Product and Engineering to deliver features that empower our Operations teams. I partner closely with Product to lead research on opportunities and define requirements. In addition to providing design solutions, I'm heavily involved in conversations around improving and implementing processes that span across multiple functions of the company.

If you want to hear more details about the work I'm doing at Human Interest, feel free to reach out. I'm sure we can work out an NDA-compliant process.
