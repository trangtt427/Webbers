Acuity Enterprise

Scheduling Solutions for Squarespace Users

Designed an enterprise-grade Squarespace product that allows our largest customers to manage scheduling across their entire organizations.

Business Problem

Squarespace acquired Acuity in 2021 and integrated the B2C offering into their suite of tools, but not the enterprise version. This left our largest customers with a hugely fragmented product and lack of basic management and security features.

    We faced 3 major challenges specific to our part of the business:

  • Acuity Scheduling was founded on the promise of serving small business owners—people who only use a single scheduling instance. No infrastructure existed to efficiently support bigger customers with complex needs like multiple scheduling instances and users. The Acuity Enterprise offering barely resembled a true enterprise-grade product.
  • Depending on what Acuity plan you use, the same features could live in different parts of the product, due to Enterprise being built with monolithic technology.
  • My joining the newly formed Enterprise Team marked us being a fully staffed squad (or triad, as Squarespace likes to call it). We knew we had to move fast to build up processes and relationships with stakeholders from nearly every Acuity department.

Our team's goal was to deliver an enterprise product that helps current customers scale their business, close contracts, and is unified with the rest of the Squarespace experience.

Impact

We successfully launched the new Acuity Enterprise experience in 2024, which was a requirement for SSO, one of the most highly requested features.
