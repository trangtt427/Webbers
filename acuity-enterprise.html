Acuity Scheduling was starting to invest more in their enterprise offering. I designed a platform to help large customers manage all things scheduling at scale.

Results

Closed 3 deals with healthcare and retail customers

Enabled single sign-on which increased leads

Business Problem

Acuity never fully integrated into the Squarespace ecosystem after its acquisition, leaving our largest customers with a severely fragmented product that lacked basic user management and security features.

We faced 3 major challenges specific to our part of the business:

There was no model to efficiently support bigger customers with complex needs like multiple scheduling instances and users.

Depending on what Acuity plan you use, the same features could live in different parts of the product, due to separate technologies.

The Enterprise team had to move fast to build up processes and relationships with stakeholders from nearly every Acuity department.

Our business goal was to deliver an enterprise product that helps current customers scale their business, close contracts, and is unified with the rest of the Squarespace experience.

Impact

We successfully launched the new Acuity Enterprise experience in 2024, which was a requirement for SSO, one of the most highly requested features. More on this soon to come...
