Trang is designing at Human Interest to help people in all lines of work achieve a secure financial future.

My resolve is to help people overcome technology through designs that are useful, functional, and aesthetically pleasing to use. I'm steadfast in making sure that my designs have a positive influence, currently by way of helping people plan for their retirement.

I'm comfortable shepherding features through its development, from defining and discovering user needs to crafting engineering-ready final prototypes. I'm a proponent of methodologies that help me understand users, cross-functional collaboration, and creative exploration. In my workflow, I frequently ask questions to learn more about the problem space and advocate for solutions that put users at the helm.

Previously, I built features for a data analytics platform at Incorta, implemented internal tools and designed a marketing website for Bio-Rad, and revamped an existing product while introducing industry-standard design processes and tools at Controlco.

Besides being a designer, my other alter egos include being an on and off illustrator, a fan of macabre art and Art Nouveau, an amateur cyclist, a bumbling guitarist, and a wannabe food connoisseur. But most of the time, I'm just a simple gal who'd just be happy with a nice bowl of ramen and a cat to pet.
