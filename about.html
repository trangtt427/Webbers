Hi. I'm Trang, a product designer who enjoys the simple things.

I believe that companies can create lasting value for people through design. I think creatively and execute tactfully to make sure that experiences are designed to be purposeful, just, and to help people thrive.

I found my calling for design several years ago while I was doing programming work in the buildings automation sector—where technology and innovation move a bit slower than the rest of the software world. There were better ways to do things and with better tools, I thought. That mindset of challenging the status quo incidentally put me on a path to become a product designer.

These days, I'm passionate about financial wellness and curious about the influence money has on our lives; topics I get to design around at Human Interest. Previously, I've designed products for tax accounting, data analytics, and IoT.

I'm into learning new things every day, exploring, and taking on (sometimes random) creative pursuits. My current favorite form of storytelling is through making videos.
