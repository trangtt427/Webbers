Hello, I'm Trang.

I'm a design leader who is always ready to roll up her sleeves to probe deep into the root of a complex business problem. I'm keen on deciphering what users say (and what they don't) to distill solutions that make their lives easier and drive positive impact for business.

In the last five years, my design acumen has brought market value to industries like IoT, biotech, fintech, marketing, and data analytics. I thrive when I get to lead design end to end and work collaboratively to advance solutions that are backed by extensive research. After getting alignment on designs, I can buckle down to create pixel-perfect visuals and clear documentation—interactions and behaviors all accounted for, to ensure a smooth hand-off for Engineering.

As much as I love design, I'm an artist above all else. The perfect day for me is when I'm dabbling in a digital illustration while in flowstate, nodding my head gently to the rhythm of lofi music, with my cat sleeping on my lap.
