Hi there.

My name's Trang. I'm a 90s baby; I used to own tamagotchis (both the keychain device and Gameboy game), witnessed the rise of the world wide web, and experienced having computers as part of the school curriculum for the first time in history. My curiosity of technology as a youngster paved way for me to become product designer.

While I want to say that most technologies try to improve our quality of life, the intent sometimes gets lost somewhere along the way. I like designing and thinking creatively to break down barriers that stand in the way of people and an equitable experience.

With my personal time, I try to get out and enjoy more of the California sun, draw, cook new recipes, and make donut runs.
