I strive to promote equity through Design.

Born and raised in a melting pot known as the Bay Area, I'm lucky to be surrounded by a beautifully inspiring environment and a diverse cast. This upbringing has ingrained into my work ethic to always consider others with different backgrounds than me. I get to put that into practice at Human Interest as a product designer.

I take the time to understand the crux of a problem and how it pans out in the grand scheme of things. I pick apart processes to find patterns and opportunities. I enable a design centric approach. I value creativity, communication and collaboration. My resolve is to get teams aligned on a vision towards materializing viable solutions that bring value to both users and business.

Design is a huge part of who I am, but I'm a creative first, with specialty in digital illustrations. I appreciate and find inspiration in different expressions of visual art, whether it's through video games, animation, graphic design, fashion, pop culture, etc. If you want to talk art, design, or anything else, feel free to hit me up!

Thanks for dropping in!
