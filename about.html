Hello, I'm Trang.

I'm driven by the mission to promote equity through Design. I get to do just that at Human Interest, where I'm working to help Americans save for a secure financial future.

In my journey so far, I've improved the data management experience for users at Incorta, helped build Bio-Rad's online catalog, and led the design for building automation solutions at Controlco.

I'm currently based in San Jose, grew up in the East Bay, but have spent most of my life in the Bay Area. These days, I'm drawing, playing my guitar a bit more, challenging myself with new cooking recipes, and enjoying the outdoors as much as I can.
