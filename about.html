About

Hi, I'm Trang.

I'm a firm believer that any piece of software can be made to be as delightful as they are impactful. That's why I'm on a mission to do good with my empathy and design superpower by creating products that are intuitive, inclusive, and empowering for people.

Throughout my career, I laid out the foundation for better user-centric and industy-standard design processes at Controlco, built up a design system while shipping a dozen features for Incorta, and created scalable design and development solutions at Bio-Rad. I'm comfortable handling features from end to end, including hearing out the users' painpoints, partnering with product management and engineering, creating wireframes and iterating a ton, testing and iterating some more, and then prettying things up with pixel-perfect designs.
