About Me

I'm a versatile designer who's fascinated by all the ways technology fits into our lives.

I aim to unlock a better wellbeing for those who use technology, through thoughtfully designed products and services that are uncomplicated, practical, and just. Currently, I'm at Squarespace designing to help people share their values with the world.

When it comes to creating solutions that users love, I pull out all the stops to get there.

I position design strategically; I execute at the highest level of craft and polish at every step; I collaborate with others to hone in on the best solution for the right time—all while making sure business needs are met. I've been designing for over 7 years with experience in a wide range of themes, but my sweet spot includes startups, fintech, research, and complex ecosystems.

I'm a big fan of storytelling.

In my professional work, it's how I build engagement and empathy with stakeholders, and outside of that, it's key to how I show up in the world. Whether it's photography, design passion projects, or documenting my cooking exploits—life is a little more fun when you let others in on what makes you you.

My binge watching abilities put my design prowess to shame.

I'm a curious person, so it doesn't take much for me to get entranced by niche YouTube content (lately, it's been aquarium building videos and I don't even own fish). If I discover an interesting show, I'll convert into a couch potato, without shame. My latest binge is Succession.
