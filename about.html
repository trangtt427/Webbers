About Me

As a versatile designer who's curious about the relationship between technology and our personal lives, I aim to create experiences that unlock a better quality of life. Currently, I'm doing this at Squarespace where I design to help people show up how they want to in the world.

When it comes to creating solutions that users will love, I pull out all the stops to get there. I have a penchant for positioning design strategically and executing at the highest level of craft every step of the way, all while ensuring business needs are met. My sweet spot: startups, fintech, and complex ecosystems.

I love storytelling. Professionally, I use it as a way to create engagement and build empathy, and outside of work, it's a big part of how I show up in the world. Whether it's with photos, design passion projects, or making cooking videos of my favorite Vietnamese dishes—life is a little more fun when you let others in on what makes you you.
