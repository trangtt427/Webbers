Hi, I'm Trang. I craft experiences with a keen eye for details, clarity, and purpose. Outside of being a full-time product designer, I help early startups with design strategy and hands-on design execution.

Growing up in a household of hardworking immigrant parents who fled from a war-torn country, earning a living by "making things look pretty" was fantasy.

I became a designer after realizing that I cared a lot about how things look, feel, and work. Being able to shape them so that people can have a good experience? Design really is my calling. My approach is rooted in keeping things simple, and with a focus on delivering quality solutions that strike a balance between business goals and user needs.
