About Me

I'm a versatile designer who's fascinated by how technology fits into our existence. My mission is to unlock a better quality of life for those who use technology, through products and services that are uncomplicated, practical, and just. Currently, I'm at Squarespace designing to empower and help people show up the way they want to.

When it comes to creating solutions that users will love, I pull out all the stops to get there.

I position design strategically and execute at the highest level of craft every step of the way, all while making sure business needs are met. I've been designing for 7+ years with experience in a wide range of themes, but my sweet spot includes startups, fintech, research, and complex ecosystems.

I'm big on storytelling.

In my professional work, it's a way to create engagement and build empathy, and outside of that, it's key to how I show up in the world. Whether it's photography, design passion projects, or documenting my cooking exploits—life is a little more fun when you let others in on what makes you you.
