Hello.

I'm Trang and I'm designing at Human Interest to help people in all lines of work achieve a secure financial future.

My resolve is to help people realize how rewarding technology can be through designs that are useful, functional, and aesthetically pleasing. I'm steadfast in making sure that my designs have a positive impact, currently by way of helping people plan for their retirement.

I'm comfortable shepherding features through their development lifecycle, from defining and discovering user needs to getting prototypes pixel-perfect for engineering. I'm all about methodologies that help me understand users, cross-functional collaboration, and creative exploration. In my workflow, I often ask a ton of questions to understand the problem space and to advocate for solutions that put users at the helm.

Besides being a designer, my other alter egos include being an amateur cyclist, an on and off guitarist, a wannabe food connoisseur, and perhaps my most defining characteristic above all, an artist. My lifelong passion for art has grounded me with creativity and observation skills that have extended into both my professional and personal life. These days, you can find me mostly drawing digital illustrations with my trusty Cintiq tablet.
