With 8+ years designing across a colorful spectrum of industries, products, and audiences, I make experiences that play to people's preferences.

I'm a creative product designer with a strong product sense for building at startups, scaleups, and big companies. When I share something with the world, I make sure it's exceptionally crafted, well-informed, and designed with people at the core. I strive to give my work a bigger purpose, hoping to leave a positive impact on others one delightful interaction at a time, and having fun with it.

Core Principles

Always consider the audience

The audience doesn't just mean the people you build for—it's also the folks you show up in meetings with, write documents or make artefacts for, and storytell to.

Sweat the details

The most unassuming design choice can imprint something extraordinary for users.

Embrace humor

I take my craft seriously but I don't take myself seriously. I try to inject some lightheartedness to boost morale whenever things get stressful. Laughing and having fun with others is underrated at work.
