Hello, I'm Trang.

I apply my design acumen to bridge the gap between technology and people through solutions that balance utility, functionality, and clean aesthetics.

I'm a design leader who is always ready to roll up my sleeves to probe deep into the root of a complex business problem. I'm steadfast in deciphering what users say (and what they don't) to distill solutions that make their lives easier and drive positive impact for business.

In my 5+ years of design experience, I've had the pleasure of bringing market value to industries like IoT, biotech, fintech, marketing, and data analytics. I'm comfortable leading design end to end and advocating for collaboration with the right folks along the way. After getting alignment on designs, I can buckle down to create pixel-perfect visuals and clear documentation—design interactions and behaviors all accounted for, to ensure a smooth hand-off for Engineering.

As much as I love design, I'm an artist above all else. The perfect day for me looks like this: I'm in flowstate as I dabble in a digital illustration, nodding my head gently to the rhythm of lofi music, with my cat sleeping on my lap.
