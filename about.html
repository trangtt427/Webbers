About

Hi, I'm Trang.

I’m a full stack product designer on a mission to help people overcome technology by threading together empathy, creativity, and data.

What got me into design? Playing the Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past for the first time in the 90s. The 8-bit music, the graphics, the storytelling — everything was beautifully strung together to deliver a captivating experience that shaped my early perspectives on art, design, and technology. Over two decades later, I am working as a designer to tell a slightly different story, one that draws upon the knowledge bred from that experience, to reassure people that technology can be delightful as they are useful.

I have experience designing for enterprise, startups, biotech, fintech, data analytics, and IoT.

I'm comfortable shepherding the whole process, from defining user stories to crafting pixel-perfect visuals. I am a huge advocate of users, collaboration, and creative exploration, and you'll notice this as I ask questions to learn more about the problem space and push for solutions that put users at the helm. In the end, the design is only useful if helps users can get their jobs done. Otherwise, it’s just expensive pixels and code.

Nothing makes me happier than going out into the world and capturing all of its beauty and complexities.

Whether it's hiking an easy nearby trail or traveling to a foreign country, I connect with the vibes around me to be inspired and reinvigorated. Some of my bigger travels are driven by my adventurous palate. Cheesy as it may sound (I like puns), food plays a huge part in my identity and is a great way of connecting with others. I also enjoy grunge music, drawing, playing my Nintendo Switch, and I like befriending every stray cat in my neighborhood.

Thanks for dropping by. Feel free to send a message if you're curious about me.
