Hi there.

My name's Trang. I'm a 90s baby; I used to own tamagotchis (the keychain and Gameboy game), witnessed the rise of the world wide web, and was the first generation to have computers built into our school curriculum. My love and curiosity of technology as a youngster eventually led to my career as a product designer.

I like helping people overcome the technology hassles in their lives so that they can spend more time with the meaningful things. I enjoy upskilling company-wide design literacy, tackling the complexities of building 0 to 1, and collaborating with others in pursuit of a better and more elegant way to do things.

With my personal time, I'm usually drawing, constantly making micro-adjustments to my computer set-up, trying out new recipes, or going for donut runs.
