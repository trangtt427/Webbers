About

With 8 years of experience designing across a colorful spectrum of industries, products, and audiences, I've honed my skills as a business-minded collaborator who cares deeply about bettering how we live our lives.

I live in the SF Bay Area and am working remotely at Squarespace. Previously, I worked at a few (mainly fintech) startups where I found my passion for designing within complex ecosystems.

Having been a sole designer and a part of bigger teams, I learned to be flexible in my areas of focus. I refine big ideas into durable concepts backed by users and principles of good design, create pixel perfect prototypes to support Engineering through development, and partner with Product to consider both the immediate goals and long-term vision—all while having a few laughs and celebrating the wins, big and small, along the way.

In my free time, I'm reliving my childhood through retro gaming and making videos about nature, travel, and cooking. You can check out some of my videos on YouTube.
