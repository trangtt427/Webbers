I'm Trang.

I believe that design is how businesses create lasting value for people. I apply my design aptitude to make sure that products and services are built to be purposeful, just, and to help people thrive.

I found my calling for product design while I was doing programming work in the buildings automation sector—where technology and innovation move a bit slower than the rest of the software world. There must be a better way to do things and with better tools, I thought. My pondering of status quos incidentally put me on a path to become a product designer, where I can nudge boundaries, in a good way.

Outside of work, I have a few creative hobbies: drawing, writing, and photography. My current muse is creating cooking videos of Vietnamese cuisine, which I hope to share with the world soon!
