About Me

Hi, Trang here. I've designed through 10 years of big shifts in technology and human needs. What hasn't changed is my passion for building useful, well-considered products and services that people love and swear by.

A lost soul originally half-serious about a career in medicine, I found my way into design by grit and luck. I earned my stripes at startups, distilled brain-racking complexities into simple solutions at B2B companies, and improved org-wide design strategy while executing as an occasional product design consultant.

I've designed all sorts of products for all types of audiences, and I'm still having a good time. I nerd out about the craft. I sweat the details. I stay close to real user insights. And I do all of this while trying to have fun with the people I build with—the secret sauce to delivering world-class products. I'm drawn to opportunities with systems-driven products where the I'm forced to connect the pieces and build something entirely new from the ground up.

Outside of design, I try to make the most out of the amazing weather and nature that comes with living in the SF Bay Area. Usually with my camera too.
