I strive to promote equity through Design. I'm doing exactly that at Human Interest, where I'm helping Americans save for a secure financial future.

I take the time to understand the crux of a problem and how it pans out in the grand scheme of things. I'm keen on picking apart processes to find patterns and opportunities. I enable a design centric approach. I value creativity, communication and collaboration. My resolve is to get people aligned on a vision and get us towards materializing viable solutions that bring value to both users and business.

I'm currently based in San Jose, grew up in the East Bay, but have spent most of my life in the Bay Area. These days, I'm drawing, playing my guitar a bit more, challenging myself with new cooking recipes, and enjoying the outdoors as much as I can.
