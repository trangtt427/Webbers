About

With 8 years of experience designing across a colorful spectrum of industries, products, and audiences, I've honed my skills as a business-minded collaborator who cares deeply about bettering how we live our lives.

I live in the SF Bay Area and am working remotely at Squarespace. Previously, I worked at startups where I found my passion for designing within complex ecosystems.

Having been a sole designer and a part of small design teams, I embrace the process of building and nurturing ideas from scratch. I tough out brain-numbing challenges to come up with research-backed solutions that are practical, functional, and delightfully simple for users—all while encouraging a few laughs along the way.

My ideal work environment is one that fosters a culture of diverse wits, collaboration, humility, and growth. Nothing beats teaming up with quirky folks who are excited about making real impact. I enjoy mentoring and helping others discover the ways design can be useful outside of building products. I believe one of the best uses of a design-forward mindset is when people apply it in their personal lives to problem-solve towards an improved quality of life.

In my free time, I'm reliving my childhood through retro gaming, painting digitally, traveling, enjoying nature, pretending like I know what I'm doing in the kitchen, and making videos about those last three. You can check out some of my videos on YouTube.
