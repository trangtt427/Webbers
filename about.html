About me

I'm a curious, big-picture thinker and strategic-minded designer who doesn't fret to ask the hard questions. I enjoy digging deep into people's motivations so I can find ways to help them thrive. But it's not all serious and strictly business with me— I'm known to sprinkle in some humor and lightheartedness throughout the sometimes stressful world of product development.

One of my quirks is that I love to experiment with different creative outlets. Whenever I'm overflowing with inspiration and energy, I'll have fun rotating between drawing, photography, and playing the guitar (I'm only half-decent in one and awful in the other two).
