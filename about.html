I'm Trang,

a product designer who's fascinated by the interweaving of technology and our personal lives. While sometimes there's badmouthing of technology, there are people who depend on it for things like health, finance, communication, and expression. I design these products and services to be just, purposeful, easy to use, and for those who earnestly want to wield it, harmlessly, for an improved quality of life. I'm getting to do a lot of that at Human Interest, where I'm designing to help small businesses make retirement attainable for their employees.

I love storytelling and being able to share my interests and experiences with the world. Whether it's through photos, design passion projects, or making cooking videos of my favorite Vietnamese dishes—life is a little better when you let others in on what makes you you.
