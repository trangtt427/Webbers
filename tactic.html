Design at Tactic

I joined Tactic as a consultant in 2021 and took on Founding Designer responsibilities of ushering a product to market while establishing the company's Design function. We were a small but mighty team of 4 after I joined, but were already getting interest from notable companies and investors. Design needs were plenty. Outside of the app, I also handled all things design-related for Marketing. As the first and only designer, my contributions included:

  • Preparing interactive prototypes and research prompts for user testing
  • Creating and evolving a design library for efficiency and consistency
  • Aligning the team and upleveling company literacy around design processes and operations
  • Shaping the Tactic identity and creating all brand-related assets

One of the biggest accomplishments was that we came out of stealth in May 2022. Unfortunately, the specifics of my work can't be shared at the moment. If you're curious to learn more, I can provide something that is NDA compliant, so feel free to reach out.
