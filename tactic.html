Tactic

accounting for web3

Designing to save businesses time with automated accounting workflows for their digital assets

With the growing popularity of cryptocurrency on balance sheets these days, businesses are needing better accounting tools to prepare for the upcoming tax season. There aren't a lot of modern solutions in the market yet, but Tactic enables these businesses to understand and report on their crypto activity, especially for huge amounts of transactions.

overview

Empowering businesses with clean and compliant solutions for tax

I joined Tactic as a part-time consultant in 2021 and took on Founding Designer responsibilities of bringing a product to market while establishing the company's Design function. We were a small but mighty team of 4 after I joined, but were already getting interest from notable companies and investors. The team didn't shy away from taking on the industry's thorniest problems. Most of the challenges were complex and nuanced workflows involving huge amounts of transactions. I was involved in designing solutions end to end, but as the first designer at the company, I also contributed to areas outside product development, including:

  • Preparing interactive prototypes and research prompts for user testing
  • Establishing and evolving a design library to promote a visual design language
  • Aligning the team and maturing company-wide literacy around design processes and operations
  • Shaping Tactic's brand identity and creating all collateral for marketing

The company came out of stealth in May 2022 (read the Techcrunch article here). If you're interested in learning more about what I'm working on at Tactic, feel free to reach out!
