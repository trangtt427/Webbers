Tactic

accounting for web3

Designing to save businesses time with automated accounting workflows for their digital assets

Despite a growing number of companies putting cryptocurrency on their balance sheets, many people dread the headaches and possible penalties that come with tax season. Tactic makes it easy for accountants, controllers, and other key finance stakeholders to understand the landscape of their crypto activity and helps them get what they need for accurate reporting of financials.

overview

The Cryptocurrency Platform Built to Save Businesses Time

I joined Tactic as a Product Design Consultant in December 2021 with the goal of elevating Design and helping the widely underserved crypto accounting community. We were a small but mighty team of 5 when I joined, but were already getting interest from big companies and investors thanks to the team's grit, user-first ethos, and eagerness to take on the industry's thorniest challenges. Our contributions to the space is to build confidence in the web3 world and help businesses save time and money. I'm involved in the entire product design process end to end, but as the first Designer at the company, I wear plenty of hats to help out wherever I was needed. Some of things I do at Tactic include:

  • Applying a Design Thinking approach to find a path forward when it comes to understanding what our users want and what features to prioritize
  • Preparing designs for early user testing
  • Establishing and evolving a design library to ensure a visual design language
  • Aligning the team and maturing company-wide literacy around Design
  • Creating brand assets and collateral for marketing and press releases

The company came out of stealth in May 2022 (read the Techcrunch article here). If you're interested in learning more about what I'm working on at Tactic, feel free to reach out!
