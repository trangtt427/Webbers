Tactic

accounting for web3

Designing to save businesses time with automated accounting workflows for their digital assets

With crypto making more of an appearance on company balance sheets these days, business controllers and other key finance stakeholders are needing better accounting tools to prepare for tax season. Tactic offers advanced, automated workflows to help businesses stay compliant. No more headaches. No more unforeseen tax penalties.

overview

Design at Tactic

In December 2021, I joined Tactic as the founding designer to drive a solution that helps businesses manage their crypto accounting. We were a small team of 4 when I had joined, but were already taking on the industry's toughest but most prevalent problems. I was responsible for designing the entire product while establishing the company's Design function, which included research efforts, upskilling company-wide understanding of Design Thinking, and building our design system. I also had the privilege of working closely with the team to shape the Tactic brand and identity into what it is today.

Most of the complex design challenges we faced were around use cases involving huge volumes of data— how might we allow users to resolve conflicts in their transactions? Should updating a rule to categorize transactions happen rectroactively? I worked with accountants to understand industry-specific workflows like chart of accounts, journaling, and reconciliation. We also honed in on user decision-making behavior to help lower the barrier of entry for setting up wallets and exchanges. While the product is tailored for businesses, we take up a consumer-friendly approach towards ensuring a product experience that is simple to use, but includes adequate educational content and guidance for anyone to catch on.
