About Controlco

Controlco integrates systems for centralized building automation controls. They are a B2B company specializing in IoT products that lets users see trending data and operate their facilities. These products are in-browser applications where users can log in to access their building's innerworkings. Controlco serves corporations that own malls, high-rises, and data centers.

Project Overview

A real estate client wanted solution where they can manage the operations of their facilities. The application would also function as a repository for files and documents pertaining to these facilities. They asked for their website to look more like a dashboard where all the important information is shown at first glance.

Methods

The project kicked off with a meeting with stakeholders and data modelers. We were given an early stage mockup that represented the vision of our clients. After getting the information we needed, I created a more detailed wireframe that would show the flow between screens.

A simple blueprint of the client's website

The mockup was refined and reiterated using feedback from our clients at every step. After several revisions, we finally compromised on the UI.

I chose to go with a white UI because it was reminscent of an excel spreadsheet (which was partially what they wanted)

With the UI design approved by the client team, I went on to building the prototype in DGLux5.

The Challenge

The most difficult part of any process is when others don't understand the workflow. The clients had high expectations and didn't know the difference between mockups and prototypes.

The issue was resolved by creating a PDF of screens for them to see and giving our clients access to the DGLux5 prototype. With those assets presented to them, they then understood the design process and our workflow. The also realized that things couldn't be turn over as quickly as they wanted because a lot of factors were involved.

The high fidelity mockup that was delivered to the UI engineering team

Results

While the process was rocky with some misunderstandings along the way, it brought some good news in the end. The clients loved the final design so much that they wanted to use this UI for all of their properties. This meant that over 30 properties will be integrated by Controlco and use this template.
